Best and Worst Leadership Speakers

In the Area of Women’s Leadership and Empowerment

Leadership speakers serve the crucial role of inspiring, developing, and motivating leaders. They leverage their experience and expertise to engage audiences through storytelling, practical frameworks, and interactive discussions. They distill complex leadership concepts into simple and actionable takeaways. Effective speakers connect with audiences by customizing their messaging to the industry, audience, and customer.

The core functions of leadership speakers include:
  • Inspiring through compelling personal narratives
  • Providing practical tools and models for improvement
  • Energizing audiences around opportunities
  • Mentoring on critical leadership capabilities
  • Promoting new mindsets, perspectives, and behaviors
  • Creating engaging, thought-provoking experiences
  • Using relevant examples and case studies
  • Discussing trends shaping the future of leadership
  • Establishing credibility and trustworthiness

Selection and Ranking of Speakers

Speakers are ranked according to the cumulative score that each speaker earned based on the following criteria:
  • Ability to engage and inspire audiences across all demographics
  • Ability to tailor the engagement to meet the specific needs of the client and audience
  • Ability to provide practical tools, and models for improvement, and actionable information which are able to be applied easily, immediately, and effectively by audience members
  • Likelihood of being invited back to speak to same audience
  • Cost effectiveness
